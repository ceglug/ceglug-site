Asst. Professor Bama Ma'am

As the world shrinks and as technology expands,

With free knowledge abundant as situation demands;

Ready sources to dip into, in this information age

And together build the world as one global village;

Connected by web and accessible by everyone

Created by us, nurtured by all, but excluding none -

Through this initiative that aims for the sun,

Let us join, learn, contribute and enjoy all the fun

Free knowledge, free sharing, and unrestricted access -

Of us, By us, and for all is our only mantra for success!

To implement new ideas and fearlessly share,

This is the place, this is the camaraderie: Come here friends.We surely care.

Let us all join, break new grounds and hoodwink luck.

This is the place! This is the site! This is IT! And this is what we call CEGLUG!
